About the service

Is my resume written by AI?

No. Every resume is reviewed and written by an experienced HR professional who reads your intake, understands your career story, and makes strategic decisions about how to position your experience. We use every modern tool available to deliver the best possible result — just like every professional does today.

How do you know what hiring managers want?

Because we've been on that side of the table. Our strategist spent over a decade inside HR at Fortune 500 companies — reviewing thousands of applications, running recruiting processes, and making hiring decisions. We don't guess what works. We know.

How is this different from other resume services?

Most resume services plug your information into a polished template. We do career repositioning — rebuilding your narrative around impact, not duties, specifically for the roles you're targeting. The difference shows up in your interview rate, not just how your resume looks.

Who is this service for?

We specialize in mid and senior-level professionals with 10+ years of experience who are re-entering the job market after a layoff, career transition, or years at the same company. If your resume hasn't been updated in a while and the hiring process feels foreign, you're exactly who we built this for.

The process

What do I need to submit?

After payment you'll complete a short intake form. You'll upload your current resume, share your target role, describe your top career accomplishments, and optionally link to specific jobs you're targeting. That's everything we need to get started.

Do I need to get on a call?

No. The entire process is async. Pay, complete the intake form, receive your documents. No calls, no scheduling headaches, no back-and-forth. Everything is handled through our structured intake process.

How long does it take?

Standard delivery is 48 hours from the time you complete your intake form. Need it faster? Add our 24-hour rush delivery option at checkout for an additional $20.

What format will I receive my documents in?

Your rewritten resume will be delivered as a Word document (.docx) so you can easily edit and customize it going forward. If you've selected the LinkedIn package, your LinkedIn content will be delivered as a document with each section clearly labeled for easy copy-paste.

Pricing & guarantees

What's included in each package?

The Basic package ($150) includes a full resume rewrite — rebuilt from scratch for today's hiring process. The Premium package ($225) includes everything in the Basic plus a complete LinkedIn profile overhaul: headline, About section, and experience rewritten and aligned to your target roles.

What if I'm not happy with the result?

We stand behind our work. If your rewritten resume doesn't feel like a meaningful improvement over what you submitted, reach out within 5 days of delivery and we'll make it right.

Is payment secure?

Yes. All payments are processed through Stripe, one of the world's most trusted payment processors. We never see or store your card information.
