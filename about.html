Most resume services are built by writers. We were built by someone who lived inside the hiring process — who knows what a recruiter sees in the first 7 seconds, what an ATS flags before a human ever reads a word, and what language actually signals senior-level impact to a hiring manager.

ResumeRewritten exists because experienced professionals deserve better than a template. After years of watching qualified candidates get filtered out because their resume didn't reflect their actual value, we decided to do something about it.
